General Health Tips

Nutrition

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit processed foods and added sugars.

Fitness

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week, plus strength training.

Sleep

Prioritize 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night. Good sleep regulates hormones that control hunger and metabolism.
