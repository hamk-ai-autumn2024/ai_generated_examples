How to Play

  • Click on a piece to select it (highlighted in blue)
  • Green dots show valid moves, red borders show captures
  • Click on a valid move to make your move
  • Pawns promote to Queens automatically when reaching the last rank
  • Check is indicated by a red border around the king
  • Game ends when checkmate or stalemate occurs
